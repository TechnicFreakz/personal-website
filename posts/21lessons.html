21 Lessons for the 21st century

by Juval Noah Harari

His third book, after "A brief history of humankind" and "Homo Deus", focuses on the present. He condenses the insights he gained from writing abut the past and the future as well as discussing all the topics that popped up during this journey with colleagues and readers, into 21 lessons for the present.
