Fourth Wing is a book about Violet Sorrengail, a daughter of a mother who is a known and feared warrior. In a medieval-style world with magic and dragons, she was supposed to be a scholar, but her mom decides for her to become a dragon rider half a year before the final decision has to be made. The book states that romance is a big part of the story.
